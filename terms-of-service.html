Terms of Service

Last updated: January 1, 2025

These Terms of Service ("Terms") govern your use of OK Car Shipping LLC ("Company," "we," "us") services and website. By requesting a quote or booking a shipment, you agree to these Terms.

1. Broker Relationship

OK Car Shipping LLC is a licensed freight broker (MC-987654), not a carrier. We arrange transportation on behalf of shippers by contracting with independent motor carriers. The carrier, not OK Car Shipping LLC, is responsible for the physical transport of your vehicle.

2. Quotes and Pricing

All quotes are estimates based on current carrier availability, fuel prices, and market conditions. Final pricing may differ from initial estimates. Any price changes will be communicated before a carrier is dispatched. You have the right to decline any revised quote without penalty.

3. Payment Terms

Payment is due at delivery directly to the carrier unless otherwise agreed in writing. Accepted forms of payment include cash and cashier's check. Credit card acceptance varies by carrier. OK Car Shipping LLC collects a broker fee which may be incorporated into the total quoted price.

4. Cancellation Policy

See our separate Cancellation Policy for full details. In general: cancellations before carrier dispatch are free; cancellations after dispatch may incur a fee.

5. Limitation of Liability

OK Car Shipping LLC's liability as a broker is limited to our broker fee. We are not liable for carrier delays, vehicle damage, or consequential losses. Damage claims must be filed against the carrier's cargo insurance.

6. Governing Law

These Terms are governed by the laws of the State of Oklahoma, United States. Disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Oklahoma County, Oklahoma.

7. Contact

Questions about these Terms: dispatch@okcarshipping.com or (405) 555-1234.
