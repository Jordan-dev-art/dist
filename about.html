About OK Car Shipping LLC

Oklahoma's Trusted Auto Transport Broker Since 2010

OK Car Shipping LLC was founded in Oklahoma City in 2010 with a simple mission: make vehicle transport transparent, reliable, and stress-free for everyday consumers and businesses alike. What started as a small regional broker has grown into a nationwide operation dispatching thousands of vehicles every year to and from all 50 states.

As a licensed freight broker (MC-987654 / USDOT 2345678), we don't own trucks — we operate a carefully vetted network of 12,000+ independent motor carriers across the country. This model allows us to offer competitive pricing on virtually every route while maintaining the flexibility to match your shipment with the carrier best suited for your vehicle, timeline, and budget.

Our Core Values

  • Transparency — No hidden fees. No bait-and-switch. Every quote includes a full breakdown.
  • Reliability — We confirm carrier identity, insurance, and FMCSA authority before dispatch.
  • Accountability — If something goes wrong, our team is available Mon–Sun to help resolve it.
  • Local Roots — Based in Oklahoma City, we understand the regional shipping corridors better than national brokers without local presence.

What We Do

We broker auto transport services including open car shipping, enclosed transport, door-to-door delivery, expedited shipping, motorcycle transport, classic car shipping, and specialty services for boats, RVs, and heavy equipment. We serve individual consumers, dealerships, auction houses, corporations, military families, snowbirds, and college students.
