What Is Motorcycle Shipping?

Motorcycle Shipping is a specialized service requiring carriers with the right equipment and permits. Our network includes carriers certified for oversized loads, motorcycles, boats, RVs, and heavy equipment, ensuring compliance with state regulations and route restrictions.

At OK Car Shipping LLC, we've been arranging motorcycle shipping for over 15 years. Our dispatchers understand which carriers are best suited for your specific vehicle and route, ensuring you get the right equipment at a competitive price.

How Motorcycle Shipping Works

  1. Request a Quote — Submit your origin ZIP, destination ZIP, vehicle year/make/model, and preferred transport method. You'll receive an instant estimate.
  2. Book Your Shipment — Review the quote and confirm your booking. No deposit is required until a carrier is assigned to your shipment.
  3. Carrier Assignment — Our dispatch team matches your shipment with the best available carrier from our vetted motorcycle shipping network.
  4. Vehicle Pickup — The carrier arrives at your location. A thorough condition inspection and Bill of Lading is completed before loading.
  5. Delivery — Your vehicle arrives at the destination. Final inspection ensures the condition matches pickup. Payment is collected at delivery (cash, cashier's check, or card).

Motorcycle Shipping Pricing

The cost of motorcycle shipping depends on several factors:

  • Distance — Longer routes cost more in total but less per mile. Short routes under 300 miles may have minimum fees.
  • Vehicle Size — Standard sedans are the base rate. SUVs, trucks, and vans cost 10–25% more due to space and weight.
  • Route Demand — Popular lanes (e.g., Florida, California, Texas) offer more carrier availability and better pricing. Less-traveled routes may cost more.
  • Season — Snowbird season (Oct–Dec, Feb–Apr) and summer moving season drive up demand and pricing. Book early for best rates.
  • Timeline Flexibility — Flexible pickup windows allow us to match you with the best-priced carrier. Tight deadlines require expedited pricing.

Frequently Asked Questions — Motorcycle Shipping

How much does motorcycle shipping cost?

Motorcycle Shipping rates range $350–$800. The final price depends on distance, vehicle size, route availability, and your requested pickup window. Use our free quote tool for an exact estimate.

How long does motorcycle shipping take?

Transit time varies by distance. Short hauls (under 500 miles) take 1–3 days. Mid-range (500–1,500 miles) takes 3–6 days. Cross-country (1,500+ miles) takes 7–10 days. Expedited options are available.

Is motorcycle shipping available in all 50 states?

Yes. Our carrier network covers all 50 states. Some rural or remote locations may require terminal-to-terminal arrangements, but we'll advise you during the quote process.

What if my vehicle is damaged during motorcycle shipping?

All shipments are covered by the carrier's cargo insurance. We also document your vehicle's condition with a detailed Bill of Lading at pickup. If damage occurs, our customer support team guides you through the claims process.
